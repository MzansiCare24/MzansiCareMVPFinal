About MzansiCare

Our Mission

MzansiCare empowers every person in South Africa with fast, fair, and reliable access to primary healthcare. We reduce queues, improve clinic operations, and give patients digital tools that put them in control.

Our Vision

We envision a connected healthcare system where clinics run smoothly, patient histories are always at hand, and reminders and supply insights enhance outcomes—across townships, cities, and rural communities.

What We Do

  • Digital Clinic Cards and health profiles
  • Appointment booking with smart reminders
  • Clinic supply tracking to prevent stockouts
  • Feedback loop for hygiene and quality of care

Privacy & Trust

We protect your data with secure best practices. Your information belongs to you—shared only with your consent and your care providers.

Built for SA, by SA

MzansiCare is designed for the realities of South African clinics—variable connectivity, high patient volumes, and diverse community needs.

Community First

We collaborate with clinic staff and patients to shape features that matter most: shorter wait times, fewer stockouts, and simpler follow-ups.
